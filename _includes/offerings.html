My Offerings

Couples Coaching

This is for couples who:

  • Love each other but feel stuck in recurring conflict
  • Want deeper intimacy but don’t know how to access it
  • Feel the polarity has faded
  • Struggle with communication breakdowns
  • Are navigating life transitions (engagement, pregnancy, parenthood, career shifts)
  • Or simply want to create something extraordinary — not average

How we do it

  • Structured communication tools
  • Repair and conflict resolution skills
  • Emotional safety & nervous system co-regulation
  • Rebuilding trust, attraction, polarity
  • Blend of somatic & parts work
  • Conscious long-term relationship design

This is not traditional talk therapy.

It is relational skill-building, pattern interruption, and intentional partnership design.

Liberation in relationship means:

  • Conflict that leads to connection
  • Honest conversations without escalation
  • Feeling chosen, respected, and desired
  • A relationship that feels alive — not stagnant

1:1 Coaching

This is for individuals who know there’s more waiting for them—more joy, more purpose, more aliveness—but feel held back by old patterns or a sense of disconnection. If you’re ready to stop just going through the motions and start living with intention, this is for you.

How We Do It:

  • We practice the Four P’s: Pleasure, Play, Presence, and Permission—bringing intention to everyday acts.
  • We blend somatic work and parts work, helping you reconnect with your body and inner wisdom.
  • We transform routine into ritual, turning daily self-care into empowerment and liberation.

What Liberation Means:

It’s about presence—no longer rushing through life. It’s turning everyday moments into intentional acts and manifesting a life beyond your wildest dreams—one you actively create.
